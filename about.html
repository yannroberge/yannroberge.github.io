About Us

Arbortronics Control Systems Inc. (ACSI) is an authorized sales distributor and Service (warranty) Centre for MEI, Sodeco, Cashcode, NRI, and Money Controls unattended payment products including coin acceptors, changers, bill validators, recyclers, BNA, BNR and wireless card payment devices. ACSI also provides service and support for other manufactured payment products including Coinco payment devices and Standard Change Machines.

The ACSI facility has been in operation since 1989 and our factory trained and certified technicians utilize PC based testing hardware enabled by manufacturer and ACSI developed diagnostic software tools to provide service and support to our customers.

Through its research and development team, ACSI designs, develops and manufacturers a wide range of PCB based interface, metering and control products.

History:

Arbortronics was established in 1989 by Motek Szpindel (P.Eng.), as a consulting company with a unique focus on analyzing and resolving field problems for complex microprocessor based equipment. The operations rapidly expanded to include design, service, installation and maintenance, light assembly and contract manufacture of electronic sub-assemblies. In the early 1990’s Arbortronics became Mars Corporation’s (MEI) first service center in Canada and later expanded to providing support to a wide range of unattended payment products in the Canadian market.

Today our sales, service and manufacturing operations encompass a wide range of commercial electronics, payment devices and industrial controls across many industries in North America including vending, laundry, retail, transportation, gaming, parking and water metering.
